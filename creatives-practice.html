SAUL BASS

During his 40-year career Saul Bass worked for some of Hollywood's most prominent filmmakers, including Alfred Hitchcock, Otto Preminger, Billy Wilder, Stanley Kubrick and Martin Scorsese. Among his most famous title sequences is Preminger's The Man with the Golden Arm.
